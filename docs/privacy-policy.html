隐私政策

Kiroku Camera · 最后更新：2026 年 2 月 8 日

Kiroku Camera（以下简称"本应用"）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了本应用如何处理您的信息。

数据收集与存储

本应用不收集、不上传、不共享任何用户数据。所有照片、记录项和元数据均存储在您的设备本地，不会传输到任何服务器。

设备权限

本应用会请求以下权限，仅用于核心功能：

  • 相机：用于拍摄进度对比照片。
  • 照片图库（读取）：用于从相册选择照片添加到记录项。
  • 照片图库（写入）：用于将生成的对比图保存到相册。

您可以随时在系统设置中撤销这些权限。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

数据安全

由于所有数据仅存储在本地设备上，数据安全取决于您设备自身的安全机制（如密码、面容 ID 等）。

儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集任何儿童个人信息。

隐私政策变更

如本政策发生变更，我们将在此页面更新内容及日期。建议您定期查看。

Privacy Policy

Kiroku Camera · Last updated: February 8, 2026

Kiroku Camera ("the App") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection & Storage

The App does not collect, upload, or share any user data. All photos, records, and metadata are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions, used solely for core features:

  • Camera: To take progress comparison photos.
  • Photo Library (Read): To select photos from your library to add to records.
  • Photo Library (Write): To save generated comparison images to your library.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's own security features (passcode, Face ID, etc.).

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the content and date on this page. We recommend checking periodically.
