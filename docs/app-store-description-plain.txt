========== 简体中文（直接复制到 App Store Connect「描述」）==========

对比相机：拍前后照片，一键生成对比图，让变化一目了然。

用同一角度记录健身、成长、护肤、植物或任何你想追踪的变化，在时间轴里查看每次记录，用并排、滑块、透明叠加等方式做前后对比，并保存成对比图到相册或分享给朋友。

为什么用对比相机？

・ 专为「前后对比」设计
不是普通相册，而是按「记录项」整理：每个主题一个记录项（如「减脂 90 天」「多肉成长」），每次拍照自动归入时间轴，方便你随时选两张图做对比。

・ 拍照对齐更准
拍照时，第一张照片会以半透明参考图叠在取景框上，帮你对齐角度和构图，保证每次拍摄条件一致，对比更有说服力。

・ 多种对比方式
支持并排对比、左右滑动对比、透明度叠加对比，细微变化也能看得清清楚楚。满意后可直接生成对比图，保存到相册或分享。

・ 数据只在你手机里
所有照片和记录仅保存在本机，不上传、不收集个人数据，隐私由你掌控。

适合谁用？

健身与减脂、育儿成长、植物日记、护肤与发型、手作与创作、宠物日常……任何你想「看见时间带来的变化」的场景，都可以用对比相机持续记录、生成对比图。

下载对比相机，把看不见的坚持，变成看得见的对比图。


========== English（若在 App Store 添加了英文）==========

Comparison Camera — Take before & after photos, generate comparison images in one tap.

Record progress at the same angle for fitness, growth, skin care, plants, or anything you want to track. View every shot on a timeline, compare any two with side-by-side, slider, or overlay modes, then save the comparison image to your photo library or share it.

Why Comparison Camera?

・ Built for before/after
Organize by projects (e.g. "90-day cut", "succulent log"). Each photo lands on a timeline so you can pick any two and compare.

・ Align shots easily
The first photo appears as a semi-transparent overlay in the viewfinder so you can match angle and framing for consistent, comparable shots.

・ Compare your way
Side-by-side, slider, or overlay — see even small changes clearly. Generate a comparison image and save or share.

・ Your data stays on your device
All photos and records are stored locally. Nothing is uploaded or collected.

Who is it for?

Fitness and weight loss, baby milestones, plant diaries, skin and hair progress, crafts and creations, pet growth — any journey where you want to see change over time.

Download Comparison Camera and turn daily effort into clear before/after comparison images.
