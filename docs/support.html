常见问题

照片存储在哪里？

所有照片和数据都存储在你的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。删除 App 会同时删除所有数据。

如何更换记录项的封面？

进入记录项详情，点击任意照片打开大图，然后点击「设为封面」。

对比图如何保存？

在对比视图中，点击右上角「保存」按钮，先预览再保存到相册。

参考图是什么？

拍照时，系统会将该记录项的第一张照片以半透明方式叠加在取景框上，帮助你对齐构图，确保每次拍照角度一致。

FAQ

Where are my photos stored?

All photos and data are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server. Deleting the app will also delete all data.

How do I change a record's cover photo?

Go to the record detail, tap any photo to open it, then tap "Set as Cover".

How do I save a comparison?

In the compare view, tap the "Save" button in the top right, preview the result, then save to your photo library.

What is the reference overlay?

When taking a photo, the app overlays the first photo of the record semi-transparently on the viewfinder, helping you align your framing for consistent shots.
